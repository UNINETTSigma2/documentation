# Question and Answer events

The Norwegian Research Infrastructure Services (*NRIS*) has an extensive education and training program to assist existing and future users of our services. In combination with traditional training events like courses, workshops and hands-on sessions, we also have ambitions of regular Q&A-sessions.


## The EESSI-collaboration Happy Hour sessions:

European Environment for Scientific Software Installations (EESSI) offers informal weekly sessions to explore and discuss key related topics and updates:
<https://www.eessi.io/docs/training-events/happy-hours-sessions/>
