NIRD Data Peak

NIRD Data Peak is a high-performance storage service tailored for data-intensive workloads and scientific investigations. It offers a fast, reliable, and collaborative place to store and work with active research data — the data that are frequently analyzing or processing.

When to use Data Peak

Choose Data Peak if:

  • You are working on active datasets that are being processed, analyzed, or updated regularly.

  • You need fast access to data for computing, visualization, or collaborative work.

  • You need backup as a service.

See the NIRD documentation for further technical and access details.
